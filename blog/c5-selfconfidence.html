What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Both Self Awareness and Mindfulness are part of our emotional intelligence. Self Awareness is how we recognise and are aware of our personal state of mind. Mindfulness is essentially the same thing, it is when we notice change occurring within our behaviour. Both of which are necessary to evaluate oneself and are a necessary step into changing the emotions that can arise and see situation from a new perspective.
