Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I used what I had learnt from the mindfulness chapter and had a mindful conversation with my girlfriend. Often I am distracted by my surroundings or thinking of future tasks that I need to work on, so this really helped me be in the present while I was speaking with her. I felt this incredible clarity in thought, and could understand exactly what she was saying. I was able to effectively express my own feelings. My girlfriend loved having a “mindful conversation” with me - she said she felt that she had my complete attention.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Practicing mindfulness through training my insula (area in the brain that is involved in emotional intelligence) by intentionally focusing on one part of the body at a time. I tried this technique after watching the video chapter, finding myself very calm and focused after doing so.
